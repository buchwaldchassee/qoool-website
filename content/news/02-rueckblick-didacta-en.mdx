We were at the Didacta education fair in Cologne from February 20 to 24, 2024. At the booth of our partner, the start-up re:edu, we presented the idea of the QOOOL project to curious visitors to the fair. In particular, the QOOOL-Kit attracted a lot of interest from schools, universities, and colleges on site. We were also able to exchange ideas with other companies, projects, and institutions about quantum sensing and technology and have taken away many contacts and impulses. We are already looking forward to next year!
